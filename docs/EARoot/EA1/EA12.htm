<font color="#0070c0"><b><u>Definition</u></b></font><br/>An individual who is performing a role on behalf of an organization who is employing them and for whom there is a set of information that applies to them in that role (described in the attributes).  <br/><br/><font color="#0070c0"><b><u>Examples</u></b></font><br/>- Grants Manager<br/>- Project Assistant<br/>- Visit Report Reviewer<br/><br/><font color="#0070c0"><b><u>Notes</u></b></font><br/>A role is not the same as a job title; a person may perform multiple different roles simultaneously and the roles may not have an obvious relation to their job title. Note too that the application of the class is wider than the "Research Staff" class currently in BRIDG<br/><br/><br/>
