<font color=#0070C0><b><u>Definition</b></u></font><font color=#000000><br/>The representation of a geographical location, a state or nation, acting in a functional capacity for a set of Study Sites, who may or may not be physically located within it, in the context of a particular Study.<br/><br/></font><font color=#0070C0><b><u>Examples</b></u></font><font color=#000000><br/>- United Kingdom - acting as a Study Country for all study sites in the UK and Republic of Ireland for the Phase III Study of Exotocillin in community acquired pneumonia in patients over 65<br/>- France - acting  as a Study Country for all study sites in mainland France and its offshore departments, plus two sites in French-speaking Belgium for the Phase II Study of Exotomumab in treatment of rheumatoid arthritis<br/><br/></font><font color=#0070C0><b><u>Notes</b></u></font><font color=#000000><br/>A Study Country exists as a business object in the context of planning and management of specific individual studies.</font><br/>
