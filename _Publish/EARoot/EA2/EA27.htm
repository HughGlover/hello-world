<font color="#0070c0"><b><u>Definition</u></b></font><br/>A corporate entity as opposed to a person.  Like a person an organization can own things, do things and be responsible for things.  It also has an identity and a contact details.  <br/><br/><font color="#0070c0"><b><u>Examples</u></b></font><br/>- Businesses: ACME Corporation<br/>- Institutions: Good Health Hospital, US National Cancer Institute (NCI); CDISC<br/>- Departments: Good Health Hospital Pharmacy<br/>- Societies: Royal College of Surgeons<br/>- Charities: Cancer UK<br/>- Governmental: Department of Health<br/><br/><font color="#0070c0"><b><u>Notes</u></b></font><br/>In CDM identity and contact details are frequently required and in some cases an Organization is the responsible party or representative for some place or object - for example the use of Organization as a proxy for Study Site (currently under debate)<br/>
